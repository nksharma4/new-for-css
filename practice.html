Microsoft

Microsoft Corporation is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Redmond, Washington. Founded in 1975, it is the world's largest software company by revenue, famous for its Windows operating system, Microsoft 365 productivity suite, Azure cloud computing, and Xbox gaming consoles

Google

Google is an American multinational technology company specializing in online advertising, search engine technology, cloud computing, artificial intelligence (AI), consumer electronics, and software. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, it operates as a subsidiary of the holding company Alphabet Inc.

Deloitte

Deloitte is a multinational professional services network and one of the world's "Big Four" accounting firms. Founded in London in 1845, it employs over 470,000 people globally and provides audit, consulting, tax, and advisory services to major organizations.
